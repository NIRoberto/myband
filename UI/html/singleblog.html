My Journey through the ATLP.

Andela Technical Leadership Program is a nine months career accelerator program implemented by Andela in partnership with the ministry of ICT and innovation. I am going to explain my journey before and after joining this program, why I joined the program, the benefits of joining this program, the skills you will gain after completing and what I expect after finishing nine months of this program.

When I was in senior three in my secondary school, I planned to be a software engineer in the future. After I have been continuing to prepare for it in the following year in secondary. Till now also I was planning to learn software engineering at my university in order to make my dream happen.

The reason why I joined the ATLP was because of my friend who told me about the ATLP. It was good for me because it was a chance to start improving my career quickly before I start my university.

After joining the ATLP I had gained many different skills that I no longer know like on the side of professional and technical skills. For professional I learnt to develop your personality, personal branding and professional writing via email or blog post. For technical I learnt new things like GitHub, about agile development which was about (plan, design, develop, feedback, release, test) your project, and also, I learnt about JavaScript skills as you see in image below the skills you will gain in the ATLP.
